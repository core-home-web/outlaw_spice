Grilled Vegetables with Zaatar

Servings
Prep Time
00:10
Cook Time
00:12
Ingredients
Total Time

Equipment

Ingredients

  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 eggplant, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons zaatar spice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine vegetables with olive oil and zaatar.
  2. Preheat the grill to medium heat.
  3. Grill the vegetables for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally.
